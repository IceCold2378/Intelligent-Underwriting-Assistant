# Internal Underwriting Guidelines

## Loan Eligibility
- The minimum acceptable credit score is 680.
- The applicant must be at least 18 years old.
- The applicant must be a resident of the United States.

## Financial Ratios
- The debt-to-income (DTI) ratio must not exceed 40%.
- The loan-to-value (LTV) ratio for a mortgage must not exceed 80%, meaning a down payment of at least 20% is required.

## Risk Flags
- Any applicant with a previous bankruptcy must be flagged for manual review.
- The loan amount requested should not exceed $500,000 for a first-time home buyer.
